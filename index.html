Our Production Site

A State-of-the-Art
Production Hub.

VTF Cosmetics is a state-of-the-art production hub dedicated to bringing the excellence of Italian bleaching powders to the heart of North Africa. Located in Ben Arous, Tunis, our facility is modern, strategically positioned, and built with sustainability in mind.

It has been specifically designed for the large-scale manufacturing of professional hair bleaching powders, ensuring outstanding performance, product stability, and full compliance with international quality and safety standards.
